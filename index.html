Meet the Founder

The Heart Behind
Stepping Stone

Qualified, experienced, and deeply committed to teaching — our founder brings real academic depth to every class.

Ms. Percy Paulin J,
Founder & Director

Ms. Percy Paulin J founded Stepping Stone Educational Centre in June 2021 with a clear vision—to provide genuinely caring, personalized, and high-quality education to students both in her local community and through online learning platforms.

She holds both a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) and a Master of Engineering (M.E.) from a highly reputed university, reflecting her strong academic foundation and commitment to excellence in education.

In addition to leading Stepping Stone Educational Centre, Ms. Percy Paulin J serves as an Assistant Professor at Sathyabama University, where she has been teaching and mentoring students at the university level for over two years. Her experience in higher education enables her to bring advanced teaching methodologies, subject expertise, and a student-centered approach into every classroom.

A passionate educator and researcher, she has authored and published multiple research papers and books, while also guiding students in research publications, patent development, and academic innovation. Her dedication to nurturing young minds extends beyond conventional teaching, inspiring students to think critically, explore creatively, and achieve their full potential.

At Stepping Stone Educational Centre, she teaches and mentors students across Grades 1 to 12, along with specialized training in Spoken English, Python Programming, and Engineering subjects. Her teaching philosophy is built on understanding each student's unique learning style, encouraging growth with patience, and celebrating every milestone along the learning journey.

Under her guidance, numerous students have consistently achieved excellent academic results, top ranks, and outstanding scores, making Stepping Stone Educational Centre a trusted destination for quality education and holistic student development.
